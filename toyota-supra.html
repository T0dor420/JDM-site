Toyota Supra

The Supra was first introduced in 1978 as a more powerful derivative of the Toyota Celica. Over the years, it evolved into a distinct model known for its performance and sleek design.The Supra is renowned for its performance capabilities. The Mark IV, in particular, with its 2JZ-GTE engine, gained a reputation for being highly tunable and capable of producing substantial horsepower. The newer Mark V continues this tradition with turbocharged inline-four and inline-six engines.Thanks to its appearances in popular culture, motorsports success, and strong aftermarket support, the Supra has become an icon among car enthusiasts and collectors. The Supra's legacy includes not only its performance and design but also its influence on the sports car market, inspiring generations of enthusiasts and becoming a symbol of Toyota's engineering prowess. Overall, the Toyota Supra remains a beloved sports car with a rich history, combining performance, style, and technological innovation across its different generations.
