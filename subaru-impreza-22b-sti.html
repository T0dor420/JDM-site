Subaru Impreza 22B STI

The Subaru Impreza 22B STI is an iconic and highly coveted model in the Subaru lineup, celebrated for its performance, rarity, and rally heritage. The 22B is a 2-door coupe featuring widened fenders, a large rear wing, and a distinct hood scoop, emphasizing its rally-inspired design. Exclusively available in the World Rally Blue (555 Blue) color. Interior: Equipped with unique blue Alcantara seats with "STi" embroidery, a Momo steering wheel, and a unique instrument cluster. A 2.2-liter turbocharged EJ22 engine. Approximately 280 horsepower and 268 lb-ft of torque. A 5-speed manual gearbox. All-wheel drive (AWD). Bilstein dampers and Eibach springs for improved handling. Upgraded Brembo brakes for enhanced stopping power. The 22B was built to commemorate Subaru's 40th anniversary and their third consecutive World Rally Championship (WRC) Constructors' title. With only 424 units produced, it is one of the rarest and most sought-after Subaru models. The 22B is renowned for its exceptional performance on both road and track, capturing the essence of Subaru’s rally success. The Subaru Impreza 22B STI remains a legend among car enthusiasts and collectors, often fetching high prices at auctions due to its rarity and historical significance. Its design and performance continue to influence Subaru’s performance models and the automotive world’s perception of the Subaru brand. Overall, the Subaru Impreza 22B STI is a remarkable piece of automotive history, embodying the spirit of Subaru’s rally dominance and engineering prowess.
